All About Us

Founding

Borders Petting Zoo was founded on the 27th of October 2017, and since then have been refining our center to give you and your family the best day out ever. We have brought in many new and different animals to our zoo, such as our recent partnership with borders donkey sanctuary, allowing you to get the best experience ever out of this. Its not only our zoo that we love and care for, we have a large range of different walks around the area which are regularly cleaned and maintained by our friendly staff. And once youve tired out the kids with a great day out, you can come and sit down in our newly built café and enjoy one of our signature hot chocolates, or adorable animal biscuits.








Staff

Meet our friendly staff! Our friendly staff are always around site to help you out, whether its encouragement to pet a scary animal, recommendation of walks or what to get for tea, our staff are always around for you. We have handpicked every member of staff to ensure they are kind, helpful and will do whatever it takes to make sure your day is the best possible day ever. The only thing they cant do for you is improve the weather.
